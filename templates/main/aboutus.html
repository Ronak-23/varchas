ABOUT VARCHAS

Varchas is the annual sports festival of IIT Jodhpur, varchas has grown to become the largest and one of the most awaited sports festival of north west India. Varchas with it's iconic slogan vigour,valour,victory symbolises the energy and courage to achieve victory and uphold the noble spirit of the games. The fest is planned to host about 2000 students pan India and has a large number of exciting competitions and activities relevant to the theme. Varchas celebrates spirit of sportsmanship and serves as a platform to showcase the countless hours of perspiration put in by the teams to achieve excellence in their sport. So gear up yourself and your squads to witness the frolicsome Varchas
